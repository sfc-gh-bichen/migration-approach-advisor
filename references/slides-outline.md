# Exec Slides Outline

Build via the Google Workspace MCP (`mcp_google-worksp_create_presentation`).
Reuse the Snowflake brand template `14NG7RAyaULOMCsOcHTyQoZxQtROW1YWSA_bTFyvpgRo`
and brand colors: Snowflake Blue `#29B5E8`, Mid Blue `#11567F`, Purple `#7D44CF`.

Keep it to ~8 slides, exec-tier (decision-focused, not engineering detail).

1. **Cover** - "Snowflake Migration Approach Recommendation - <CUSTOMER>",
   subtitle with date + author. Split blue/white layout.

2. **Situation & driver** - source platform(s), inventory headline numbers,
   the single primary business driver. One bold takeaway.

3. **The approach spectrum** - one row, three cards: Lift & Shift -> Selective
   Refactor -> Re-architect. Effort/risk/value arrows underneath. Mark the
   recommended card.

4. **Recommendation** - Option 1 named, the one-line why, T-shirt size, and
   (if computed) ~P50. Big and unambiguous.

5. **Recommended approach across the 6 dimensions** - the 6-row table for Option 1
   with effort/risk per dimension.

6. **Options compared** - the decision matrix (3 columns) with overall
   effort/risk/value-capture rows; recommended column highlighted.

7. **Why this approach** - 3 bullets tying the choice to the customer's signals
   (driver, timeline, team maturity). Note Repurchase/Retire moves.

8. **Next steps** - confirm approach -> detailed sizing (modernization-assessment)
   -> POC slice. Thank-you / contact.
